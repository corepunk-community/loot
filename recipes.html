Corepunk has two separate crafting systems and they look the same at first glance. This page keeps them apart so you can find what you're looking for.

  • Crafting Recipes — the original profession system (Mining, Butchery, Food, etc.). Each recipe has a profession, ingredients, and a single product.
  • Synthesis Blueprints — the newer "synthesis" system that produces runes, weapons, consumables, and artifacts. Grouped by which item system they belong to.
